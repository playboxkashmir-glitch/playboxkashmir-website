About PlayBox

Where passion meets play. A modern sports destination built for athletes and enthusiasts alike.

Play On The Best Turf In The Valley

PlayBox Kashmir brings premium FIFA-standard artificial grass to Srinagar. Our floodlit arena is designed for football, cricket nets, and group sports — open early morning to late night.

Whether you're organizing a friendly match, a corporate tournament, or a weekend kickabout, our facilities deliver a professional playing experience with top-tier safety and comfort.
