Cancellation & Refund Policy

Information regarding cancellation requests, refunds and booking modifications for PlayBox Kashmir™.

Last Updated: 15 July 2026
Cancel more than 24 hours before your booking for a full refund of the booking amount. Convenience fees are non-refundable.

1. Cancellation & Refund Schedule

Refund eligibility is determined solely by the time your cancellation request is received by PlayBox Kashmir via email.

Cancellation Time Refund
More than 24 hours before the scheduled booking time 100% refund of the booking amount (excluding convenience fee)
12 to 24 hours before the scheduled booking time 50% refund of the booking amount (excluding convenience fee)
Less than 12 hours before the scheduled booking time No refund
No-show or failure to utilise the booked slot No refund

2. Cancellation Procedure

All booking cancellations must be requested exclusively by email.

Please send your cancellation request to:

contact@playboxkashmir.com

Your cancellation email must:

  • Include your Booking Reference Number.
  • Be sent from the same email address that was used to make the booking.

For security and fraud prevention purposes, cancellation requests sent from any other email address will not be accepted or processed.

Bookings cannot be cancelled through phone calls, WhatsApp, social media, in person or through any other communication channel. Only cancellation requests received by email will be considered valid.

3. Time of Cancellation

The applicable refund shall be determined based on the exact date and time your cancellation email is received in our inbox, irrespective of when it is reviewed or processed by our team.

4. Refund Processing

Where applicable, approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method used during booking.

Refunds are generally initiated within a reasonable period; however, the time taken for the refunded amount to reflect in your account depends upon your bank, card issuer or payment service provider.

5. Convenience Fee

Please Note:

Any convenience fee, payment gateway charge, platform fee or similar processing charge collected at the time of booking is non-refundable under all circumstances, including eligible cancellations.

6. Cancellation by PlayBox Kashmir™

If PlayBox Kashmir is unable to honour a confirmed booking due to maintenance, unsafe playing conditions, weather events, technical issues or other unforeseen circumstances, customers will be offered either:

  • A full refund of the booking amount; or
  • A complimentary rescheduling to another available slot.

7. Need Assistance?

For any questions regarding your booking or this policy, please contact us at:

Email: contact@playboxkashmir.com

Phone: +91 88994 49965

Please note that telephone support is available only for general enquiries. Booking cancellations are accepted exclusively via email in accordance with this policy.
